## Voice & Tone

**Voice**: Technical but accessible, confident but not arrogant
**Tone**: 
- Enthusiastic about performance gains
- Transparent about what works and what doesn't
- Helpful and educational
- Community-focused

**Do's:**
- Use concrete numbers (801%, 15.68 tok/s)
- Explain why optimizations work
- Credit llama.cpp and TurboQuant paper
- Show benchmark methodology

**Don'ts:**
- Overpromise or use marketing fluff
- Hide failed experiments
- Claim features that don't work (e.g., TQ3/TQ4)
- Ignore physics limits
